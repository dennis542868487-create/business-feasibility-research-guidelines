# Decision summary

The shop's verified operating baseline is 1-5 weekday orders and 2-7 weekend orders. The proposed 50-80 weekday range is roughly a 10-80× order-of-magnitude claim across the observed baseline interval. Regional visitor attendance cannot directly support one store's orders.

The model must establish service-area presence, free time, bike-rental need, store exposure, choice, payment, and displacement. Fulfilled demand must use a capacity ceiling: `fulfilled_orders = min(latent_orders, capacity)`.

Current evidence cannot support a large non-refundable fleet purchase, and no user-supplied maximum loss was provided. Run event-specific reservations or deposits, a landing page, and a small paid pilot with predeclared success and failure thresholds before the next decision.
