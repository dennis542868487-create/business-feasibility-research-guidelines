# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful, welcoming, and free from harassment for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Critique ideas, evidence, calculations, and code rather than people.
- Disclose relevant conflicts of interest.
- Protect confidential, personal, and commercially sensitive information.
- Accept maintainer decisions and continue disagreements without hostility.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, doxxing, deliberate disclosure of secrets or personal data, sustained disruption, and bad-faith manipulation of evidence are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates this policy. Report conduct concerns privately through the contact method on the maintainer's GitHub profile. Report security vulnerabilities through GitHub's private security-advisory feature instead of a public issue.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and other spaces where someone represents the project.
